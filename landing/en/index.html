CdA Calculator TT

Free CdA calculator (aerodynamic drag coefficient, m²) from a Garmin TCX file — power meter, speed, and barometric elevation. Robert Chung's Virtual Elevation method helps you compare TT positions, helmets, and equipment without a wind tunnel.

  • Upload a TCX file and select a route segment
  • Auto-fit CdA plus a slider to compare the VE profile
  • Continental GP5000 TT tire presets (Crr)
  • Data processed locally — your file never leaves the browser
